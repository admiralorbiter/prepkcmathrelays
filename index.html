The Annual PREP-KC Math Relays

Join us for the 10th Annual PREP-KC Junior Math Relays will be held at Ruskin High School from 8:30 am - 1 pm on March 2nd, 2023.

The PREP-KC Junior Math Relays is an annual event where students in our partnering districts all around the KC Metro compete using their math skills. This is a great opportunity for our students to demonstrate their math and problem-solving skills, which are critical components of their college and career preparation. The event will also provide students with a platform to showcase their abilities and receive recognition for their hard work and achievements while having fun doing so!
